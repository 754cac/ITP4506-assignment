About Us

Yummy Restaurant Group Limited is a catering company. It has grown into one of the largest catering company in Hong Kong. The Group provides diversified services including Chinese Restaurants, Western Restaurants, Japanese Restaurants, Conveyor-belt Sushi Restaurants, Fast Food Restaurants and etc. It has over 10 brands and around 100 restaurants in 2021.
